Analysis Depth

How I Help Process Survey Data

A survey is only as strong as its statistical validation. I ensure your raw inputs are structured to isolate genuine trends.

1. Data Cleaning & Labeling

Raw questionnaire exports from Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, or Google Forms are often messy. I clean inconsistent formats, handle missing parameters, code categorical values into numeric groups, and prepare the dataset for SPSS or R.

2. Scale Reliability (Cronbach's Alpha)

When utilizing Likert scales to measure latent traits, checking internal consistency is mandatory. I run reliability tests to evaluate whether questionnaire items successfully load onto their respective target constructs.

3. Descriptive & Frequency Reports

I generate clear distributions of respondent demographic profiles and core variables. You receive clean frequency tables and descriptive charts showing percentages, medians, and standard deviations.

4. Inferential Testing

I execute appropriate statistical tests to verify your research hypotheses. This includes chi-square tests for categorical associations, t-tests or ANOVA for group differences, and correlation matrices to identify relationships.

5. Regression Modeling

Evaluate predictive relationships within your survey. I structure linear, logistic, or ordinal regression models to determine how demographic variables or survey predictors influence your dependent indicators.

6. Report-Ready Interpretations

I compile all results into clear tables and clean charts, accompanied by concise notes explaining the statistical significance (p-values, degrees of freedom, effect sizes) so you can write your results section easily.
