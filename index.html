Research Obstacles

Stuck on your statistical results?

Many graduate students and academic researchers collect high-quality data but face bottlenecks during the analysis phase. Writing a thesis or research paper requires structured, clean statistical verification.

1
Selecting the right test: Confusion between parametric and non-parametric options (regression vs. ANOVA, chi-square models).
2
Messy dataset structures: Dealing with empty variables, raw CSV files, or mislabeled survey scales.
3
Software complexity: Fighting with SPSS syntax errors, R packages, or custom Python scripts.
4
Interpretation & formatting: Turning raw outputs into clear text summaries and APA-style tables when appropriate.
