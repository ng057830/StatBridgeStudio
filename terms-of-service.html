Terms of Service

1. Scope of Service

These Terms of Service govern the data analysis, data cleaning, statistical consulting, and reporting support services provided by StatBridge Studio. Services are conducted online based on client-provided datasets, questionnaires, and research objectives.

2. Academic Code & Ethical Conduct

StatBridge Studio is committed to strict academic ethics. StatBridge Studio will not fabricate datasets, falsify results, plagiarize content, or provide real-time assistance during exams or graded academic tests. Clients represent that they use these analysis outputs solely as analytical support and guidance to help write their own academic or professional research.

3. Scoping & Delivery

Quotes and delivery timelines are estimated based on information provided in the quote request form. Any changes to the scope, variables, or hypotheses after the project has started may affect pricing and delivery deadlines. Delivery is performed via secure email links.

4. Payments & Refund Policy

Service prices depend on the chosen package and project requirements. Payments must be processed through the designated channels. Because data analysis involves dedicated analytical work, refund requests are evaluated individually before the final files are compiled and delivered.
