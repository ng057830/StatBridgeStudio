Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

At StatBridge Studio, StatBridge Studio respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the datasets, questionnaire models, and personal details you share with StatBridge Studio. This Privacy Policy details how StatBridge Studio manages and safeguards your information.

1. Information Collection

StatBridge Studio collects information when you submit a project inquiry through the quote request form. This includes your name, email address, country, project type, dataset format, analysis preferences, and project descriptions.

2. Dataset Confidentiality

Any files or data you share with StatBridge Studio (such as Excel sheets, CSV records, SPSS files, or questionnaires) are treated with strict confidentiality. They are used exclusively to assess scope, define pricing, and run the requested statistics. StatBridge Studio never shares, sells, or publishes your data.

3. Data Retention

Once your data analysis project is completed and delivered, your datasets are retained locally for a maximum period of 30 days to facilitate revision requests. After this validation period, all customer files are permanently deleted.

4. Cookie Policy & Analytics

This website utilizes basic cookies via Google Analytics to measure site visits anonymously. You can choose to accept or reject analytical cookies through the consent banner.

5. Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy, please write to StatBridge Studio at: hello@statbridgestudio.com
