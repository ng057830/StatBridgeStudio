Academic Alignment

Support For Graduate Research

Writing a thesis or dissertation results chapter requires methodological transparency. I help you align your statistics with your methodology.

1. Hypothesis Mapping

I align your research questions with appropriate statistical models. I help you identify independent, dependent, and moderating variables to ensure your tests match the research scope.

2. Data Preparation & Cleaning

Messy spreadsheets can corrupt statistical models. I handle outliers, categorize open-ended responses, label missing observations, and format variables for import into SPSS, R, or Python.

3. Rigorous Hypothesis Testing

I execute the tests required to verify your arguments: multiple regression models, ANOVA for multi-group variances, chi-square tests for categorical associations, and correlation checks.

4. Statistical Assumption Checks

Every parametric test requires validating assumptions. I run checks for normality (Shapiro-Wilk), homoscedasticity (Levene's test), multicollinearity (VIF scores), and outliers.

5. Clean Tables & Visualizations

I structure your results into clear, report-ready tables (formatted using APA guidelines when appropriate) and produce high-resolution plots for data distribution and regression trends.

6. Clear Statistical Interpretation

I supply detailed written notes explaining the outputs: coefficient estimates, significance levels (p-values), confidence intervals, and effect sizes (eta-squared, Cohen's d) so you can write your results.
